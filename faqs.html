Forget Password showing error

If you see error while reset your password that means you didn't configure your email client . You need to config email. To config email edit .env file found at the root of the folder. You will something like this at the bottom of the file 
MAIL_DRIVER=smtp
MAIL_HOST=smtp.mailtrap.io
MAIL_PORT=2525
MAIL_USERNAME=null
MAIL_PASSWORD=null
MAIL_ENCRYPTION=null
Put your email credentials here
